    Accessibility Paragraph

    It's important to make your sites accessible as there are many people with disabilities who rely on third party sites and if your site is inconsistent, then these sites won't be able to communicate effectively to everyone. It's also important to keep text size and color in mind when designing your CSS. Making text too small or picking colors that don't have enough contrast can limit people's ability to read. Lastly, by adding alt text to images, you can describe the image for people who aren't able to see it.
